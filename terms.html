What Swoopic does

Swoopic helps you review and clean up the photo library on your device. Photos you swipe to Trash are collected in an in-app list and are only removed from your library when you confirm the purge, at which point your operating system moves them to its own Recently Deleted / Trash area (typically recoverable for about 30 days).
