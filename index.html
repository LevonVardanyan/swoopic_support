What Swoopic does

Swipe to clean

Fly through your library one photo at a time — swipe up to trash, down to keep, with instant undo and a satisfying purge at the end.

Quick Clean finders

Swoopic automatically finds your largest files, near-duplicate similar shots and blurry photos — the fastest gigabytes you'll ever free.

Private by design

All photo analysis (duplicates, blur detection) runs on your device. Your photos are never uploaded anywhere.

Smart groups

Browse by screenshots, videos, selfies, Live Photos, panoramas, bursts, years, months, places and albums.

Safe deleting

Nothing is deleted until you confirm the purge, and your OS keeps deleted photos recoverable in Recently Deleted / Trash for ~30 days.

iCloud aware

Photos stored in iCloud load progressively with download progress — clean your whole library, not just what's on the phone.
